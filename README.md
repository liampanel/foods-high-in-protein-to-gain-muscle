## Why High-Protein Foods Are Important for Muscle Gain

Protein is the building block of muscle. When you train, your muscles experience small tears. High-protein foods help repair this damage and promote muscle growth.

### Key Benefits:
- Supports muscle repair and growth  
- Improves workout recovery  
- Helps increase strength and endurance  
- Reduces muscle breakdown during training  


## Best Foods High in Protein to Gain Muscle

Below are some of the most effective protein-rich foods you should include in your diet.

## 1. Chicken Breast


Chicken breast is one of the most popular muscle-building foods due to its high protein and low fat content.

### Benefits:
- High-quality lean protein  
- Low in fat and calories  
- Easy to prepare in multiple ways  


## 2. Eggs


Eggs are a complete protein source, containing all essential amino acids needed for muscle growth and repair.

### Benefits:
- Complete amino acid profile  
- Affordable and widely available  
- Supports muscle recovery  


## 3. Salmon


Salmon is rich in protein and omega-3 fatty acids, which help reduce inflammation and support muscle recovery.

### Benefits:
- High-quality protein  
- Supports heart health  
- Reduces muscle soreness  


## 4. Greek Yogurt


Greek yogurt contains more protein than regular yogurt and is great for muscle repair and gut health.

### Benefits:
- High protein content  
- Improves digestion  
- Ideal for snacks or breakfast  


Lentils are a powerful plant-based protein source, especially useful for vegetarian muscle-building diets.

### Benefits:
- High plant protein  
- Rich in fiber and nutrients  
- Budget-friendly  

Tofu is a versatile plant-based protein made from soybeans and is commonly used in vegetarian and vegan diets.

### Benefits:
- Complete plant protein  
- Low in calories  
- Easy to cook in many dishes  

## How Much Protein Do You Need to Gain Muscle?

For muscle growth, your daily protein intake should be:

- **1.6 to 2.2 grams of protein per kg of body weight**

Example:
- 70 kg person → 112g to 154g protein per day



## Best Time to Eat Protein-Rich Foods

- **Breakfast:** Starts muscle protein synthesis  
- **Post-Workout:** Enhances recovery and growth  
- **Throughout the day:** Maintains steady amino acid levels

- Greek yogurt  

### Lunch

### Snack

### Dinner
- Salmon or tofu with vegetables  


## Tips for Faster Muscle Growth

- Eat protein in every meal  
- Combine diet with strength training

- Get enough sleep for recovery
